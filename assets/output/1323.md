# 1323. 6 和 9 组成的最大数字

🟢 <font color=#15bd66>Easy</font>&emsp; 🔖&ensp; [`贪心`](/tag/greedy.md) [`数学`](/tag/math.md)&emsp; 🔗&ensp;[`力扣`](https://leetcode.cn/problems/maximum-69-number) [`LeetCode`](https://leetcode.com/problems/maximum-69-number)

## 题目

You are given a positive integer `num` consisting only of digits `6` and `9`.

Return _the maximum number you can get by changing**at most** one digit (_`6`
_becomes_`9` _, and_`9` _becomes_`6` _)_.



**Example 1:**

> Input: num = 9669
> 
> Output: 9969
> 
> Explanation: 
> 
> Changing the first digit results in 6669.
> 
> Changing the second digit results in 9969.
> 
> Changing the third digit results in 9699.
> 
> Changing the fourth digit results in 9666.
> 
> The maximum number is 9969.

**Example 2:**

> Input: num = 9996
> 
> Output: 9999
> 
> Explanation: Changing the last digit 6 to 9 results in the maximum number.

**Example 3:**

> Input: num = 9999
> 
> Output: 9999
> 
> Explanation: It is better not to apply any change.

**Constraints:**

  * `1 <= num <= 10^4`
  * `num` consists of only `6` and `9` digits.

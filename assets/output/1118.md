## 题目

Given a year `year` and a month `month`, return _the number of days of that
month_.



**Example 1:**

> Input: year = 1992, month = 7
> 
> Output: 31

**Example 2:**

> Input: year = 2000, month = 2
> 
> Output: 29

**Example 3:**

> Input: year = 1900, month = 2
> 
> Output: 28

**Constraints:**

  * `1583 <= year <= 2100`
  * `1 <= month <= 12`

## 题目

Given an integer `n`, return _the number of ways you can write_`n` _as the sum
of consecutive positive integers._



**Example 1:**

> Input: n = 5
> 
> Output: 2
> 
> Explanation: 5 = 2 + 3

**Example 2:**

> Input: n = 9
> 
> Output: 3
> 
> Explanation: 9 = 4 + 5 = 2 + 3 + 4

**Example 3:**

> Input: n = 15
> 
> Output: 4
> 
> Explanation: 15 = 8 + 7 = 4 + 5 + 6 = 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5

**Constraints:**

  * `1 <= n <= 10^9`

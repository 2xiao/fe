> > 
> |
> 
> | 3> 
>    | 11> 
> > 
> | 1> 
> > 
> > 
> |
> 
> | 4> 
>    | 11> 
> > 
> | 7> 
> > 
> > 
> |
> 
> | 5> 
>    | 11> 
> > 
> | 7> 
> > 
> > 
> |
> 
> | 6> 
>    | 11>

> | 3>

> |

>

> 
> | driver_id | cnt |
>

> 
> | 7> 
> > 
>  | 2   |
> 
> | 11> 
> > 
> | 0   |
> 
> +-----------+-----+
> 
> Explanation: 
> 
> There are two drivers in all the given rides: 7 and 11.
> 
> The driver with ID = 7 was a passenger two times.
> 
> The driver with ID = 11 was never a passenger.
